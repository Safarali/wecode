Don't Think Bootcamp

Our Values & Ethics

You'll join the dynamic community of learners at one of our WeCode locations

Motivation First

Our selection process is based only on talent and motivation, with no consideration given to gender, nationality, ethnicity or social status.

We learn

There is no competition. All of us want to learn. You’ll collaborate with your peers and build projects in pairs and small groups. We are stronger when we are united.

We Share

We believe that we achieve and learn more when we share our knowledge. We get stronger when we give. We succeed by sharing our challenges and wins.

We Succeed

We learned, we shared, we got inspirations from each other. Definitely, we are already succeeding. Tomorrow will be created by our — We.
